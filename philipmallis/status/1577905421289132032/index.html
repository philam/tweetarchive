MetPlan 1988: a citywide transport plan from The Met (set up 1983). Lots of plans for improvements to public transport. Lots of talk of tram priority, increasing train services & new high-frequency buses called "Metlink". The patronage forecasts here are interesting in hindsight!

City of Melbourne Transport Strategy 1992: council transport strategy, the last before the 1994 amalgamations. Lots of detail about the changes happening during this time around the city, including bypass roads, bike routes & even airport rail. Lots of nice photos too!
